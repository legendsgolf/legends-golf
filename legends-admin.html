⛳ Legends Golf — Cloud Admin

Publish the tournament, watch scores arrive live, import into your leaderboards

Step 1 — Publish Tournament to Cloud

Sends your players, course and rounds to the cloud so the player app can see them. Do this after any change in the main Admin Panel (new player, round opened/closed, notice posted).

Checking local tournament data…

Step 2 — Player Live Leaderboard

When ON, players can see the live net Stableford leaderboard in their app. Turn OFF to keep results secret for the presentation. (Takes effect on players' phones within seconds.)

Live Leaderboard in Player App

Step 3 — Live Incoming Scores

Updates automatically as players enter scores on the course. ✓ Match means the player's own card and their marker's card agree.

Waiting for scores…

Step 4 — Import into Your Tournament System

Pulls all matched scorecards into this laptop's tournament data. After importing, ALL your existing pages work as normal — Stableford, Skins, Eclectic, Presentation, everything.
